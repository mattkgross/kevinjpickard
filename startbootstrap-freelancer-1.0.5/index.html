The Baja Collegiate Design Series is an annual competition which tasks collegiate engineering teams from around the world to design, build, and race a single seater UTV. Design, Build, Fly

Design, Build, Fly is an annual competition organized by the American Institute for Aeronautics and Astronautics. It tasks collegiate teams from around the world with designing, building, and flying an unmanned, electic, radio-controlled, aerial vehicle that can carry a series of payloads around a specified course. Teams are ranked based on their speed, maximum payload weight, and size or weight of the vehicle.

  • Host: AIAA DBF
  • Date: August 2012-May 2016
  • Website: Design, Build, Fly at CU Boulder

Imaging System Overhaul

During my work at the Integrated Teaching and Learning Laboratory (ITLL) I was tasked with overhauling the imaging system. The goal was to increase the flexibility of the system, with the hope of automating as much of the process as possible and decreasing the time required to build new system images. I tested, evaluated, and helped to implement the Microsoft Deployment Toolkit and Windows Deployment Services in our production environment. This not only reduced the time required to build images by more than half, but also allowed the images to be platform-independent, and the process is almost completely automatic. In addition, it is much cheaper than the previous system.

  • Client: University of Colorado Boulder
  • Date: August 2013-May 2016
  • Service: Microsoft Delpoyment Toolkit
